Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you get started

Is WorthScale really free?
Yes, completely and permanently free. The dashboard, calculators, and all tools have no hidden fees, no premium tier, and no trial period. Free forever means free forever.
Do I need to connect my bank account?
No. WorthScale does not connect to your bank, demat account, or any financial institution. You manually enter your asset and liability values. Your banking credentials stay completely private and secure.
How is my financial data kept safe?
Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest. We never sell, share, or use your financial data for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I use WorthScale on my mobile phone?
Yes. The website is fully responsive and works on all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop. No app download needed. Just open it in your browser.
What is net worth and why does it matter?
Net worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities. It's the most accurate single measure of your financial health — far more honest than salary or bank balance alone. Tracking it monthly helps you see if your wealth is actually growing.
Is this only useful if I have a high income or large investments?
Not at all. WorthScale is useful at any stage — whether you have ₹10,000 or ₹10 crore saved. The earlier you start tracking, the better your financial decisions will be. Start with exactly what you have today.
What tools and calculators are available?
We currently offer three free calculators: Net Worth Calculator (assets minus liabilities), Emergency Fund Calculator (find your ideal safety net size), and House Down Payment Calculator (plan your home purchase). More tools are being added.
