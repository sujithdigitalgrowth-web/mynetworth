Common Questions

Is WorthScale completely free?

Yes — completely and permanently free. The dashboard, all calculators, market pages, and blog content are free with no hidden fees, no premium tier, and no sign-up required for most features.

Does WorthScale show live market prices?

Yes — WorthScale displays live index levels and crypto prices, refreshed approximately every 5 minutes. For real-time tick-by-tick data, use NSE (nseindia.com), BSE (bseindia.com), or your broker app. WorthScale is a finance education and analysis platform, not a live trading terminal.

Who is the richest person in India in 2026?

Mukesh Ambani, Chairman of Reliance Industries, is India's richest person in 2026 with an estimated net worth of approximately $115 billion. Gautam Adani of the Adani Group is a close second at ~$63 billion. See our full India Rich List 2026 for the complete rankings.

What is net worth and why does it matter?

Net worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities. It is the most honest single measure of financial health — more meaningful than salary or bank balance. Tracking it monthly shows whether your wealth is actually growing or just your income. Use our free net worth calculator to find your number in 2 minutes.

Is crypto legal in India?

Yes. Cryptocurrency is legal in India — it is not banned. However, gains are taxed at a flat 30% rate with 1% TDS on transactions. Trading must be done on FIU-registered exchanges. See our complete India crypto guide for full details on regulations and how to start.
