Frequently Asked Questions

Is WorthScale really free?
Yes — completely and permanently free. The dashboard, calculators, and all tools have no hidden fees, no premium tier, and no trial period.
Do I need to connect my bank account?
No. WorthScale does not connect to your bank or demat account. You manually enter your values — your banking credentials stay completely private.
How is my financial data kept safe?
Your data is stored in Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your financial data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What is net worth and why does it matter?
Net worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities. It's the most accurate single measure of your financial health — far more honest than salary or bank balance alone. Tracking it monthly helps you see if your wealth is actually growing.
