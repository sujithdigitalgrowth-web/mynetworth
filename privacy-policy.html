Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data WorthScale collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use the WorthScale Dashboard, you may provide:

  • Name and email address — used to identify your account and sync data
  • Financial data — assets, liabilities, goals, insurance details, and other financial information you enter into the dashboard
  • Contact form messages — name, email, and message content when you reach out to us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Device and browser information — browser type, operating system, screen size
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, features used
  • IP address — for security and analytics purposes

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service — your financial data is stored in Firebase Firestore to enable cloud sync across devices
  • To improve the product — anonymous usage analytics help us understand which features are useful
  • To respond to inquiries — if you contact us, we use your email to reply

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your personal or financial data to third parties
  • We never share your individual financial information with anyone
  • We don't use your data for targeted advertising
  • We don't access your bank accounts, demat accounts, or any external financial systems

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, which provides:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Google Cloud's security infrastructure
  • Data centers with physical security, access controls, and monitoring

Your account is identified by email address. We use client-side processing — your financial calculations happen in your browser, not on our servers.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Local storage — to remember your login session and preferences
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous usage tracking to improve the product (can be blocked via browser settings)

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — data storage and sync. Subject to Google's privacy policy
  • Vercel — website hosting. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible in the dashboard
  • Delete your data — you can clear all your data from the dashboard settings, or contact us for full deletion
  • Export your data — use the dashboard's export feature to download your information
  • Opt out — you can use the calculators and blog without any account or data collection

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on some public pages of this website (calculators, blog posts, and informational pages). The dashboard experience is ad-free.

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party advertising partners (including Google) may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect or receive information from this website and elsewhere on the internet and use that information to provide targeted advertising services.

We do not use your personally identifiable financial data for advertising purposes. Ad personalisation is based on your browsing behaviour, not on the financial information you enter in the dashboard.

Children's Privacy

WorthScale is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact
