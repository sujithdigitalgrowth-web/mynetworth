What Does Jio Own?

Jio Platforms houses the telecom services (~75–80% of revenues) and digital services (~20–25%). Telecom: JioFiber (broadband), JioAirFiber (5G fixed-wireless), postpaid/prepaid wireless, enterprise connectivity. Digital: JioCinema (acquired Disney+ Hotstar India in 2024), JioSaavn (music streaming), JioMart (e-commerce), MyJio, JioMeet (video conferencing), JioGames.

The 2025 Nvidia partnership for AI infrastructure ("AI everywhere for everyone") positions Jio as a cloud and AI provider — going beyond telecom into AI-as-a-service for Indian enterprises and consumers.
