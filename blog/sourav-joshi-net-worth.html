How Sourav Joshi Built His Fortune

Sourav Joshi from Haldwani, Uttarakhand, started with an art channel (Sourav Joshi Arts) in 2017 focused on sketching and drawing, which built a niche 28M+ subscriber base. He launched Sourav Joshi Vlogs in 2019 and the channel grew slowly until 2020 when the COVID-19 lockdown prompted him to start daily vlogging. The 365-day daily vlog challenge became the breakthrough — he documented everyday life in Haldwani in a relatable, family-oriented format that had broad appeal across Tier 2 and Tier 3 India.

By 2022 his channel crossed 20 million subscribers; by 2024 it had nearly 40 million, making it one of the largest personal YouTube channels in India. The entire family became part of the content: father Harish, mother Hema, brother Sahil Joshi (also a vlogger), and cousin Piyush Joshi (one of Uttarakhand's most famous vloggers). Bigg Boss 17 in October 2023 extended his reach to national television audiences.

The Family Vlogging Empire

The Joshi family from Haldwani collectively represents one of the largest regional vlogging networks in India. Piyush Joshi (Sourav's cousin) built an independent 10M+ subscriber channel covering local Uttarakhand life. Sahil Joshi (brother) has a separate channel. The ecosystem has made Haldwani one of the most YouTube-mapped cities in India, and collectively the family earns significant joint brand revenue from being packaged together in integrations.
