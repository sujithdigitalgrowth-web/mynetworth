The Founders: Aman Gupta and Sameer Mehta

Aman Gupta (CMO) was previously at KPMG and JBL India (Harman International) before starting boAt in January 2016 with Sameer Mehta. His ₹720 crore personal net worth comes from his equity stake in Imagine Marketing (boAt's parent company). Gupta is also a Judge on Shark Tank India (Seasons 1–4), which massively amplified boAt's consumer brand visibility.

Sameer Mehta (CEO/CPO) holds approximately 24.75% pre-IPO stake. The two began by solving a problem Aman saw every day in airports: Apple Lightning cables breaking at the neck after a few months.

What Makes boAt Different

boAt launched in January 2016 with Apple-compatible charging cables. Today it sells TWS earbuds, over-ear headphones, speakers, smartwatches, gaming peripherals, and home audio systems. The brand's positioning — affordable, accessible, youth-targeted, cricket-backed (team sponsorships) — proved a winning formula against Sony, JBL and Bose at the sub-₹3,000 price point.

In March 2026, Aman Gupta separately launched OFF/BEAT Studios — a B2B audio production studio venture backed by Bessemer Venture Partners with ~₹100 crore seed funding. This is entirely separate from boAt.
