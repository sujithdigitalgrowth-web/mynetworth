How Hrithik Roshan Built His Fortune

Hrithik debuted in Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai (2000), the Rakesh Roshan-directed phenomenon that made him an overnight superstar at age 26. Koi Mil Gaya (2003) launched the Krrish franchise (2006, 2013), establishing him as Bollywood's only home-grown superhero star. Dhoom 2 (2006), Jodhaa Akbar (2008), and Bang Bang (2014) cemented his commercial dominance through the 2000s and 2010s.

The wealth inflexion was the 2013 launch of HRX activewear with Myntra as the exclusive distribution partner. The brand has scaled to multi-hundred-crore annual revenue and ranks among India's largest homegrown activewear labels by 2026, with valuations ranging ₹200 crore to ₹7,300 crore depending on source. War 2 (August 2025) became his second-highest grosser in India, beating Krrish 3. Krrish 4 has been confirmed by Rakesh Roshan with pre-production starting early 2026, filming mid-2026, and a 2027 release target. Hrithik may make his directorial debut on Krrish 4.

Income Sources in May 2026
