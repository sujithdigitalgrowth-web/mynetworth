The Paytm Timeline: IPO Crash to Comeback

2000: Vijay Shekhar Sharma founds One97 Communications in Delhi after graduating from DCE (now DTU). Father was a schoolteacher in Aligarh.

2010: Paytm launched as a mobile prepaid recharge platform. "Paytm karo" becomes a household phrase post-demonetisation 2016.

2018: Berkshire Hathaway invests $300M — Warren Buffett's first direct investment in an Indian company.

Nov 18, 2021: Largest Indian IPO — raised ₹18,300 crore at $19B valuation. Listed down 27% on day one — worst IPO listing day in Indian history.

Jan–Feb 2024: RBI imposes severe restrictions on Paytm Payments Bank operations. Business disruption and massive stock sell-off.

Apr 24, 2026: RBI cancels Paytm Payments Bank licence entirely. VSS surrenders all ESOPs Mar 2025; takes ₹4 Cr/year salary for FY26–FY28.

FY26: Despite bank closure, One97 Communications posts 3 consecutive profitable quarters on financial services and payment aggregator revenue.
