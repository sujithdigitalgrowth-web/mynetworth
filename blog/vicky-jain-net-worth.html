How Vicky Jain Built His Fortune

Vicky Jain was born into the Jain business family of Chhattisgarh, where Mahavir Inspire Group operates coal-trading, coal-washery, and power-generation businesses. After completing an MBA from JBIMS Mumbai, he returned to Chhattisgarh to manage and expand the family group into real estate, logistics, education, healthcare, and diamonds. The business' dominant sector is coal and allied power infrastructure, where Chhattisgarh is one of India's largest production hubs.

National visibility came through Bigg Boss 17 (October 2023–January 2025) alongside wife Ankita Lokhande. The couple had won Smart Jodi in 2022. Laughter Chefs Season 2 (2025) followed, with Vicky reportedly earning ₹1.2 lakh per episode. The September 2025 announcement of his debut as a film producer — Chandani Bar, releasing 3 December 2026 — marks his entry into entertainment business.
