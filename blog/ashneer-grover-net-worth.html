How Ashneer Grover Built His Fortune

The IIT Delhi → IIM Ahmedabad → Kotak Investment Banking → American Express → Grofers path was textbook before co-founding BharatPe in 2018 with Shashvat Nakrani. BharatPe reached unicorn status by 2021 at USD 2.85 billion. The Shark Tank India Season 1 (2021-22) appearance made Grover's catchphrases culturally ubiquitous. His 2022 autobiography Doglapan became a Penguin bestseller. The forced exit from BharatPe in March 2022 amid a board investigation was a setback, but residual BharatPe equity (held in Resilient Growth Trust and family trust per the September 2024 civil settlement) preserved a substantial portion of the wealth. Third Unicorn launched in 2023 as the post-BharatPe vehicle.
