How Raj Shamani Built His Fortune

Raj Shamani from Indore was born into the Shamani family which runs Shamani Industries, an FMCG company producing household cleaning products with approximately ₹200 crore turnover. He moved to Mumbai and began creating content on personal finance and entrepreneurship around 2019–2020. The Figuring Out podcast quickly rose through the ranks, differentiating itself by landing global guests — from Indian startup founders to international athletes and political leaders.

In 2026 he was declared India's #1 podcaster, with the show ranked #1 on Spotify India and YouTube. Key guests include Bill Gates, Michael Phelps, Vijay Mallya, and the President of France. Invitations followed to the UN (2025 speaker) and Davos-equivalent events. Alongside podcasting, he co-founded House of X with Neeraj Kumawat as a creator economy infrastructure play — managing and monetising creators at scale.
