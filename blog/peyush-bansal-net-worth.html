How Peyush Bansal Built His Fortune

Peyush Bansal graduated from IIT Delhi and went to the US for his MBA, landing at Microsoft as a program manager from 2007 to 2008. He built SearchMyCampus in 2007 while still at Microsoft — a platform for buying, selling, and renting campus-related goods. In 2010 he returned to India and founded Lenskart, an online eyewear marketplace. The business grew through the omnichannel model, opening physical stores with IPL-quality brand advertising. SoftBank invested in 2019, Temasek and Alpha Wave in 2022, and a secondary round in 2024. The November 2025 IPO was the culmination.
