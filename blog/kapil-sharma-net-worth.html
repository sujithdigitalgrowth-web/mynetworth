How Kapil Sharma Built His Fortune

Kapil Sharma's break came in 2007 with The Great Indian Laughter Challenge Season 3, which he won. Comedy Nights with Kapil launched on Colors in 2013 and made him a household name. The Kapil Sharma Show on Sony followed in 2016, becoming one of India's most-rated comedy shows of the late 2010s. Each platform shift came with a meaningful pay rise.

The wealth inflexion was the 2024 Netflix deal. The Great Indian Kapil Show launched as his first OTT-exclusive vehicle, paying a reported ₹5 crore per episode. Across Seasons 1 (13 episodes), 2 (13 episodes), 3 (14 episodes), and Season 4 in 2025–26, the cumulative fee has crossed ₹200 crore. The OTT model works because Netflix pays both for the comedy draw and the global Indian-diaspora audience appeal.
