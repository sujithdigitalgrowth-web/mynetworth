The Bundl to Swiggy Origin Story

December 2013: Sriharsha Majety and Nandan Reddy (both BITS Pilani) launch Bundl — a hyperlocal courier platform in Bengaluru.

August 2014: Pivot to food delivery. Rahul Jaimini joins as a key technology co-founder. The app focuses on restaurants that do not have delivery staff.

2017–2021: Series rounds from Naspers, Tencent, SoftBank, Coatue. By 2021, Swiggy is a decacorn valued over $10B in pre-IPO rounds.

2020: Instamart launched — grocery and essential delivery. Competes with Zomato's Blinkit (acquired) and Zepto.

November 13, 2024: IPO at ₹390/share. Market cap at listing approximately ₹87,000 crore.

2025–26: Revenue growing but losses continue. Focus on Instamart profitability and B2B supply chain services.
