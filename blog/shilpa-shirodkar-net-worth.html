How Shilpa Shirodkar Built Her Wealth

Shilpa Shirodkar debuted in Bollywood in 1989 with Bhrashtachar and quickly became one of the leading actresses of the early 1990s. Her notable credits include Henna (1991), Khuda Gawah (1992) opposite Amitabh Bachchan, Aankhen (1993) with Govinda, and Mrityudaata (1997). She was among the top-paid actresses of her era. In 2000, she married Aparesh Ranjit and retired from acting to join him in the UK, where the family lived across New Zealand, Netherlands, and ultimately settled in London.

The household wealth today is predominantly the product of Aparesh Ranjit's two-decade career at Barclays, combined with their London property appreciating significantly over the same period. The couple's combined financial status became widely discussed during Bigg Boss 18, where reports suggested Shilpa was the wealthiest contestant in the house.
