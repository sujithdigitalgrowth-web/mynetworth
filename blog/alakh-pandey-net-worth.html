How Alakh Pandey Built His Fortune

Alakh failed IIT-JEE and dropped out of HBTI Kanpur. He began teaching physics in a Prayagraj tuition setup, then started 'Physics Wallah' on YouTube in 2014–2016. The early years generated minimal income. Free, accessible JEE and NEET content built deep audience trust among middle-class aspirants who could not afford Kota coaching. The June 2020 PW app launch with co-founder Prateek Maheshwari marked the first monetisation inflexion.

What This Means for Tracking Your Own Wealth

Alakh Pandey's story is the equity-concentration risk made visible. When 97% of your net worth is in one listed stock, every 10% market correction wipes ₹1,600 crore off your notional wealth. For a salaried professional, the parallel is having 97% of savings in one company's ESOP. The lesson from PW is not that equity concentration is bad — it builds the billion — but that once built, diversification matters.
