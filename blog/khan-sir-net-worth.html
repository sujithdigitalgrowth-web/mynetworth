How Khan Sir Built His Fortune

Khan Sir's path is one of the most distinctive in Indian education. He began coaching circa 2008-2010 in Patna, focused on government-job aspirants for BPSC, UPSC, SSC, and Railway exams. The Khan GS Research Centre brick-and-mortar coaching business scaled steadily through the 2010s. The YouTube channel launched in 2018-2019 added a digital flywheel that became transformational during the COVID-19 lockdown of 2020.

Lockdown-era live GS and current-affairs explainers in his signature Bhojpuri-influenced Hindi went viral, taking the channel from a few hundred thousand subscribers to multiple millions within months. By 2022, he had become the dominant vernacular educator for BPSC and UPSC content. The May 2025 secret marriage to AS Khan, revealed live in class during Operation Sindoor, drew attention from Bihar's political leadership including Governor Arif Mohammad Khan, Deputy CM Samrat Choudhary, and Tejashwi Yadav at the 2 June 2025 reception.
