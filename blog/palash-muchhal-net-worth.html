How Palash Muchhal Built His Fortune

Palash Muchhal is the younger brother of playback singer Palak Muchhal and brother-in-law of composer Mithoon. The Muchhal family shifted to Mumbai in 2006 from their native Indore for film-industry opportunities. Palash earned a Guinness World Record in 2014 for Youngest Professional music composer, an early career milestone that established his industry standing.

His Bollywood debut came with Dishkiyaoon (2014) which featured the hit 'Tu Hi Hai Aashiqui'. Further work spanned Bhoothnath Returns (2014), Miss Tanakpur Haazir Ho (2015), and Sweetiee Weds NRI (2017). Music director credits expanded into Kaagaz (2021), Aazaad, and Mooh Dikhayi. The directorial debut came with Ardh (2022) starring Rajpal Yadav and Rubina Dilaik. In early 2026, he announced a new film with Shreyas Talpade.
