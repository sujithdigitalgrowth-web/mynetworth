What Went Wrong

The Collapse in Numbers

  • Q3 FY26 revenue fell -55% QoQ — from a high of 84,029 deliveries to 32,680
  • Market share erosion: TVS iQube, Bajaj Chetak and Ather 450 took significant share
  • Product quality complaints and service centre shortages hurt brand reputation
  • Emkay Global cut target to ₹20 citing "doubts on survival" as a going concern
  • Citi maintained sell rating with ₹27 target
  • Net debt: ₹670 crore (end FY26) — manageable but combined with losses, a concern

The Recovery Thesis

Bhavish Aggarwal's "V-shaped" recovery argument rests on three pillars: (1) Gigafactory in Tamil Nadu — now at 2.5 GWh capacity, scaling to 6 GWh in 2026 for LFP cell in-house production to cut battery costs; (2) Roadster motorcycle deliveries beginning — a premium product targeting a new demographic; (3) Krutrim AI — separately, his AI venture is gaining attention, though it doesn't directly help Ola Electric's finances.
