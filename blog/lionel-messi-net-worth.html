How Lionel Messi Built His Fortune

Messi joined Barcelona at age 13 in 2000 and made his senior debut in 2004. The next 14 years at Barcelona produced four Champions League titles, ten La Liga titles, and a record eight Ballon d'Ors. Peak Barcelona contract reportedly paid him EUR 71 million per season net. The December 2022 World Cup win with Argentina was the career-defining trophy. The July 2023 move to Inter Miami restructured the economics: lower base but Apple TV+/MLS revenue-share, Adidas integration, and equity-purchase rights. The October 2025 extension through 2028 cemented the model.
