How Deepika Padukone Built Her Fortune

Deepika started with Aishwarya (2006 Kannada debut), broke through with Om Shanti Om (2007), pivoted to mature roles with Cocktail (2012), and built a sustained franchise with Bajirao Mastani (2015) and Padmaavat (2018). The Hollywood dip with xXx: Return of Xander Cage (2017) established international brand value. The blockbuster era began with Pathaan (2023), Fighter (2024), and Kalki 2898 AD (June 2024) — each crossing ₹1,000 crore globally. She is the only Indian actress with three such films. KA Enterprises (founded 2017) and KA Productions run alongside the film career. 82°E skincare launched 2022, raising USD 13.5M total.
