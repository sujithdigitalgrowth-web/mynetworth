How Shah Rukh Khan Built His Fortune

Shah Rukh Khan arrived in Mumbai in 1991 with no film-industry family. Deewana (1992) launched him. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995), Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998), and Mohabbatein (2000) defined the 1990s romantic-hero era and made him globally recognisable across diaspora markets. He co-founded Red Chillies Entertainment in 2002. The KKR acquisition in the inaugural IPL auction in 2008 was among the most consequential investments of his career — KKR won the title in 2012, 2014, and 2024, building the Knight Riders franchise into a five-country cricket brand.

The 2023 comeback — Pathaan (January), Jawan (September), and Dunki (December) — generated ₹400–500 crore in personal earnings after years of flops (Jab Harry Met Sejal, Zero, Aanand L Rai's Dwarf). The 2023 trifecta was the largest single-year income event of his career.
