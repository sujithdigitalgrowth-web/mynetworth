How Vivek Oberoi Built His Fortune

Vivek Oberoi's Bollywood career began with Company (2002) and Saathiya (2002) to immediate acclaim. However, after a high-profile personal controversy around 2003, his film career trajectory was disrupted. Rather than depend on Bollywood, Oberoi pivoted aggressively into business. Karrm Infrastructure was founded in 2011 to develop affordable housing in Mumbai's outer corridors and Karjat. BNW Real Estate Developments followed as a UAE-focused co-venture. Solitario Lab-Grown Diamonds was co-founded to capture the global shift toward synthetic diamonds. The business portfolio essentially replaced the film career as the primary wealth engine over 2015–2026.
