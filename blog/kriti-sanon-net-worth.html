How Kriti Sanon Built Her Fortune

Kriti Sanon holds a BTech in Electronics and Communication from Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune. She started modelling and won the Yamaha Fascino Miss Diva 2013 contest before being cast in Heropanti (2014) opposite Tiger Shroff for a debut that won her a Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut. Her career accelerated through Dilwale (2015), Bareilly Ki Barfi (2017), Luka Chuppi (2019), and Arjun Patiala (2019).

The career-defining breakthrough was Mimi (2021) — a surrogate mother role for which she trained in prosthetics, underwent extensive physical transformation, and won the National Film Award for Best Actress in 2023. Post-Mimi, her brand value escalated significantly. Adipurush (2023) and Crew (2024) maintained visibility. The Hyphen skincare co-founding in 2023 marked her transition from endorser to brand owner.

Hyphen Skincare: The ₹400 Crore ARR Brand

Hyphen was co-founded by Kriti Sanon in 2023 in partnership with PEP Technologies, which is the parent company of mCaffeine. By July 2025, Hyphen reached ₹400 crore ARR, tripling from its launch revenue in approximately two years. The brand has 100+ SKUs covering face serums, moisturisers, sunscreens, and body care. Distribution spans Nykaa, Tira, Amazon, Flipkart, and the Hyphen direct website, with 4 million customers. At ₹400 crore ARR, the brand's equity value at standard D2C multiples is likely ₹1,000–2,000 crore, though Kriti's stake percentage is not publicly disclosed.
