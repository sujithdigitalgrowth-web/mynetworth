How Radhika Gupta Built Her Career and Wealth

Born in Pakistan to an Indian diplomat father, Radhika Gupta grew up across multiple countries before studying BSc Economics at the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School. After Wharton, she worked at McKinsey in the US, then at AQR Capital in New York (one of the world's largest quant hedge funds). In 2009, she returned to India and co-founded Forefront Capital Management with Nalin Moniz and Anant Jatia — focused on systematic/quant investing. Edelweiss Financial Services acquired Forefront Capital in 2014. She was appointed CEO of Edelweiss Mutual Fund in 2017.

Under her leadership, Edelweiss MF went from being an also-ran AMC to one of the fastest-growing in India. AUM grew from ₹91,000 crore in October 2022 to ₹1.64 lakh crore in October 2025, an 80-plus percent growth in three years. The Shark Tank India platform from 2024 onwards transformed her into a household name beyond the finance world.
