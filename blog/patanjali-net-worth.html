Balkrishna vs Ramdev: The Real Power Behind Patanjali

The Ownership Structure Most People Miss

  • Acharya Balkrishna: ~94% stake in Patanjali Ayurved; net worth ~₹29,680 Cr ($3.5B)
  • Baba Ramdev: Brand ambassador, face, and yoga guru — zero equity stake
  • Balkrishna's lifestyle: No salary, works 15+ hours/day, lives monastically
  • The legal structure keeps Ramdev's public persona separate from the corporate entity
  • This structure also means controversies around Ramdev have limited direct corporate liability

Controversies and Legal Battles

In 2020, Patanjali claimed Coronil was a cure for COVID-19, drawing regulatory pushback. In February 2024, the Supreme Court of India banned all Patanjali misleading advertisements and held Balkrishna in contempt. Fourteen product licences were revoked by Uttarakhand authorities. By August 2024, the SC closed contempt proceedings after both Ramdev and Balkrishna tendered an unconditional public apology.
