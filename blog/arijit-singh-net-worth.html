How Arijit Singh Built His Fortune

Singh was the runner-up on Fame Gurukul reality show in 2005 at age 18. The next six years were spent in low-paid background-singer and arranger work in Mumbai studios — a period of quiet grind that is frequently cited as foundational. The breakthrough came in 2011 with 'Phir Mohabbat' from Murder 2. The wealth inflexion was 2013's 'Tum Hi Ho' from Aashiqui 2 — the song that made him India's most-streamed and most-booked playback singer almost overnight. The subsequent decade has been a compounding exercise: songs become streams, streams become royalties, royalties compound into the corpus.
