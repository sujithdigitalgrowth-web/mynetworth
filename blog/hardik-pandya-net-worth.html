How Hardik Pandya Built His Fortune

Hardik Pandya grew up in Surat, where his father ran a small car-finance business. He dropped out of school in Class 9 to train for cricket. The IPL pathway was transformative: picked up by Mumbai Indians at the 2015 auction for ₹10 lakh — the minimum bid. By IPL 2022, the Gujarat Titans franchise paid ₹15 crore as captain, and he won the IPL that year. The return to Mumbai Indians in 2024 as captain (replacing Rohit Sharma) was controversial with fans but financially decisive — the MI retention at ₹16.35 crore confirmed the franchise's commercial calculus. The T20 World Cup 2024 final over in Barbados against South Africa was the sporting peak that validated the investment.
