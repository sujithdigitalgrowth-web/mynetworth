How Kylie Jenner Built Her Fortune

Kylie Jenner debuted on Keeping Up with the Kardashians in 2007 at age 9. The reality-TV exposure made her a global teenage celebrity by the early 2010s. The 2015 launch of the Kylie Lip Kit (her first beauty product) was the entry point into the cosmetics business. Kylie Cosmetics launched in 2016 and scaled rapidly through influencer-marketing and direct-to-consumer e-commerce. By 2018, Forbes featured her on the cover claiming youngest self-made billionaire status at age 21.

In January 2020, Coty Inc acquired 51 percent of Kylie Cosmetics for USD 600 million, valuing the company at approximately USD 1.2 billion. Kylie retained 49 percent. In May 2020, Forbes stripped her billionaire title after alleging the family had inflated the cosmetics company's financials. Kylie Skin launched in 2019, the Khy fashion line in November 2023, and Sprinter (vodka soda) in 2024. The 2026 Khy relaunch with full creative control and the k2o by Sprinter beauty-led hydration line marked the next chapter.
