How Lenskart Built Its Moat

Vertical Integration — Lenskart's Competitive Edge

  • 109-person in-house design team creates all frames
  • Own manufacturing facilities in Bhiwadi, Gurugram, Singapore and UAE
  • Brands: John Jacobs + Owndays (premium), Vincent Chase, Lenskart Air, Hustlr, Hooper Kids

The IPO Story

Lenskart listed on November 10, 2025 at ₹402/share, raising ₹7,278 crore in India's first major eyewear IPO. The stock had a tepid debut (opened ₹395, hit a low of ₹356.10 on day 1, closed at ₹404.55). But as FY26 numbers came in strong (+38% revenue growth), the stock rallied ~28% to ₹517 by May 2026. Investors who bought at IPO are up ~28%; those who held from the first-day low have done better.
