How Aman Gupta Built His Fortune

Aman's path is the structured-finance to founder trajectory. CA from ICAI, MBA from ISB/Kellogg, stints at Citigroup and as Director of Sales at Harman International (JBL India). The Harman role taught him the consumer-audio category that would anchor boAt. He co-founded boAt with Sameer Mehta in 2016, scaling it into India's largest audio brand within five years through D2C and quick-commerce channels. Shark Tank India Season 1 (2021) made Aman a household name and became a secondary wealth platform for deal flow.
