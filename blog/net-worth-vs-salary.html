Why Your Net Worth Matters More Than Your Salary

If you are serious about building wealth in India, you need to stop focusing only on salary and start tracking your net worth. Your income shows how much you earn — your net worth shows how much you actually have.

Most people measure success using salary. But two people earning the same salary can have completely different financial lives. One builds wealth, the other stays stuck in debt. The difference is net worth.

The Salary Illusion

A high salary can create the illusion of wealth. But if expenses are high and savings are low, your net worth may remain zero or even negative.

Net Worth vs Salary

  • Salary = Income
  • Net Worth = Wealth

Salary stops when you stop working. Net worth continues to grow if managed correctly.

Why Net Worth is the Real Metric

  • Shows actual financial progress
  • Helps in long-term planning
  • Measures wealth, not lifestyle
  • Gives financial security

Lifestyle Inflation Problem

As income increases, spending also increases. This prevents wealth creation.

If your expenses grow at the same rate as your salary, your net worth will never grow.

How to Shift to Net Worth Thinking

  • Track your net worth monthly
  • Invest before spending
  • Reduce unnecessary EMIs
  • Focus on long-term growth

Financial Freedom Formula

Monthly expenses: ₹80,000
Yearly: ₹9.6L
Required corpus: ₹2.4 crore

This is how financial freedom works — not based on salary, but net worth.
