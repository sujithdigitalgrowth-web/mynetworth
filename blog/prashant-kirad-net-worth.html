How Prashant Kirad Built His Fortune

Prashant Kirad's path is one of the cleaner Indian edtech-creator stories. Born in Alwar, Rajasthan, he completed B.Tech in Computer Science from NSUT Delhi after clearing IIT-JEE on his first attempt. After briefly taking a conventional job, he pivoted to teaching and launched ExpHub on YouTube around 2018–2019. The COVID-era online-learning boom from 2020 onwards drove rapid scale, with the channel crossing 1 million subscribers within two years.

The ExpHub format combines free YouTube content for CBSE Class 9–12, JEE, and NEET aspirants with paid crash courses for higher-intent students. The dual-funnel model converts free viewers into paying customers, a structure pioneered in India by Physics Wallah but executed at smaller scale by ExpHub. By early 2026, the channel had crossed 11 million subscribers, ranking among India's largest school-exam edtech channels. Prashant has reportedly mentored 1 million-plus students cumulatively across his free and paid content.
