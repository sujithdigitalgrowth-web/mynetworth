📖 What is Net Worth?

Net worth is a financial snapshot of your life. It is the value of everything you own minus the value of everything you owe. This simple formula gives you a much clearer picture of your financial health than salary alone.

Total Assets
What you own
−
Total Liabilities
What you owe
=
Net Worth
Your wealth

A positive net worth means your assets are higher than your liabilities. A negative net worth means your liabilities are currently larger than your assets. That is not uncommon in the early stages of a career, especially if you have loans. What matters most is improving the number steadily over time.

🧾 How to Calculate Net Worth in India — Step by Step

Here is the simplest way to calculate your net worth in India:

  1. List all your assets

    Include savings accounts, fixed deposits, mutual funds, stocks, EPF, PPF, NPS, gold, property, land, vehicle resale value, and any other meaningful assets you own.

  2. List all your liabilities

    Include home loan outstanding, car loan, personal loan, education loan, credit card dues, gold loan, and other debts you still need to repay.

  3. Subtract liabilities from assets

    Use the simple formula: Net Worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities. The result is your current financial position.

  4. Track it every month

    Calculating net worth once is useful. Tracking it monthly is powerful. It shows whether you are actually building wealth or just earning money.

🚀 How to Improve Your Net Worth

There are only two ways to improve net worth: grow your assets and reduce your liabilities. The most effective wealth builders do both at the same time.

💸

Increase Your Savings Rate

The more of your income you keep, the faster your net worth grows. Focus on increasing surplus every year.

🔥

Eliminate High-Interest Debt

Credit card dues and personal loans destroy wealth. Paying these off is often the fastest way to improve net worth.

📈

Invest Consistently

SIPs in mutual funds, long-term equity investing, and disciplined saving help assets grow steadily over time.

🏠

Build Appreciating Assets

Focus more on assets that can grow in value such as equity, long-term investments, and quality property.

🛡️

Protect Wealth with Insurance

A medical emergency can wipe out years of savings. Health and term insurance help protect net worth.

📅

Track Monthly

Monthly tracking helps you stay aware, motivated, and honest about your financial progress.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is net worth?
Net worth is the total value of your assets minus your liabilities. It shows your actual financial position.
How do I calculate net worth in India?
Add all your assets like savings, mutual funds, stocks, EPF, PPF, property, and gold. Then subtract all liabilities such as loans and credit card dues.
What is a good net worth in India?
A common benchmark is 1–3 times annual income by age 30, and higher as you grow older. But consistent upward growth matters more than a fixed number.
Does salary matter more than net worth?
No. Salary tells you how much you earn, but net worth tells you how much wealth you have actually built.
How often should I calculate net worth?
Once a month is ideal. Monthly tracking helps you understand your progress and improve your financial decisions.
