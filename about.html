About NetWorth

A free personal finance platform built to help Indians understand, track, and grow their wealth — simply and privately.

Our Mission

Financial clarity for every Indian household

Most Indians don't know their net worth. They know their salary, maybe their bank balance — but not the complete picture. We built NetWorth to change that: one honest number, tracked monthly, available to everyone for free.

Why We Built This

Most Indians have no idea what their net worth is. They know their salary, maybe their bank balance, but not the full picture — what they own versus what they owe.

We built NetWorth because we believe everyone deserves to know their financial position clearly. Not through complicated spreadsheets or expensive apps — through a simple, free tool that takes 5 minutes to set up.

The goal is straightforward: help you see where your money is, understand where it's going, and make better decisions about how to grow it.

Our Values

  • 🆓
    Free forever. No premium plans, no paywalls, no trial periods. The full product is free because financial awareness shouldn't cost money.
  • 🔒
    Privacy first. Your data is yours. We use Firebase for secure cloud sync, and we don't sell or share your financial information with anyone.
  • 🇮🇳
    Built for India. Indian rupee formatting (lakhs and crores), Indian investment types (PPF, EPF, NPS, ELSS), and content tailored to Indian financial planning.
  • 🎯
    Simple over complicated. No jargon, no 50-field forms, no unnecessary features. Just what you need to track and improve your finances.
  • 📖
    Education included. Our blog and calculators help you learn as you go. We want you to understand your money, not just track numbers.

Built With

NetWorth is a lightweight web application — no downloads, no installations. It runs entirely in your browser and syncs to the cloud via Firebase. The tech is intentionally simple so the app loads fast, works on any device, and stays reliable.

  • Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks
  • Firebase Firestore for cloud sync
  • Chart.js for visualizations
  • Hosted on Vercel for fast global access

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind NetWorth?
NetWorth is built and maintained by a small team of personal finance enthusiasts in India. We built this because we couldn't find a simple, free, India-first tool to track personal net worth. So we made one.
How does NetWorth make money if it's free?
We may display non-intrusive advertisements on some public pages (calculators, blog, landing pages). The dashboard experience is ad-free. We never sell user data. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but all existing free features will remain free.
Is NetWorth a SEBI-registered financial advisor?
No. NetWorth is a personal finance tracking and education platform, not a registered financial advisor. Our calculators and content are for informational purposes only. For personalized financial advice, please consult a SEBI-registered advisor.
Can I request a new feature or calculator?
Yes! We build based on what users actually need. If there's a calculator or feature that would help you, send us a message on the Contact page. Popular requests get built first.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can clear your data from the dashboard settings. For complete account deletion, contact us at hello@mynetworth.in and we will permanently delete all your data within 7 business days.
